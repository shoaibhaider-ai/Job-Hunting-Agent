ROLE OVERVIEW
-------------

- Write SQL queries to extract and join data from relational databases.
- Prepare reports and presentations summarising analytical findings.
- Collaborate with the BI team to integrate insights into Tableau dashboards.

REQUIRED SKILLS

- Proficiency in Python, especially pandas and numpy.
- Working knowledge of SQL (MySQL or PostgreSQL preferred).
- Familiarity with machine learning workflows and scikit-learn.
- Experience with data visualization libraries (matplotlib, seaborn).
- Basic understanding of statistics (mean, median, distribution, correlation).
- Strong communication and presentation skills.
- Team collaboration and problem solving mindset.

PREFERRED SKILLS
----------------
- Exposure to Tableau or Power BI.
- Experience with Jupyter Notebook for iterative analysis.
- Knowledge of data preprocessing and feature engineering.
- Familiarity with GitHub for managing analysis notebooks.
